Site Name

The name I'm going to use for the website is Haiti Heritage. This name emphasizes both Haiti’s historical richness and cultural heritage, inviting tourists to experience it firsthand. It speaks to the country's deep-rooted traditions, from its iconic landmarks like the Citadelle Laferrière to the vibrant art, music, and festivals that are woven into daily life

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to highlight Haiti’s profound historical and cultural heritage, offering travelers a unique window into the nation’s vibrant and resilient spirit. Our goal is to inspire tourists to go beyond typical vacation destinations by uncovering the rich legacy and traditions that define Haiti. Through curated guides, stories, and travel tips, we aim to showcase everything from the island’s historic sites—such as the awe-inspiring Citadelle Laferrière and the ruins of Sans-Souci Palace—to the colorful festivals, music, art, and cuisine that embody Haitian culture. This site invites visitors to experience Haiti’s warmth, connect with its people, and explore the landscapes and history that make it one of the Caribbean’s most captivating destinations. Whether you're interested in adventure, relaxation, or cultural discovery, we’re here to guide you every step of the way in exploring Haiti’s unique charm and storied past.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I’m planning my first trip to Haiti and want to learn about the must-see historical sites and cultural experiences. What are some top recommendations, and how can I make the most of my visit?"

Purpose: To guide first-time visitors in exploring Haiti’s key historical landmarks, such as the Citadelle Laferrière, and recommend cultural activities like local art tours, food markets, and festivals.

Scenario 2:

"I’m interested in eco-tourism and sustainable travel. What natural attractions does Haiti offer, and how can I support local communities during my stay?"

Purpose: To provide information on Haiti’s natural attractions—such as hiking trails, beaches, and caves—and offer tips on how visitors can travel sustainably, support local businesses, and contribute positively to the environment and communities.

Scenario 3:

"I’m a history enthusiast looking to understand Haiti's fight for independence and its cultural impact. Where can I learn about significant historical events and visit related sites?"

Purpose: To cater to visitors interested in Haiti’s rich history, offering details on historical tours, museums, and key locations tied to Haiti’s independence and cultural heritage, like the monuments and museums in Cap-Haïtien.

Scenario 4:

"I want to experience authentic Haitian food and local nightlife. What are the best places to eat, and where can I go to enjoy live music and traditional dances?"

Purpose: To highlight popular dining spots that serve authentic Haitian cuisine and recommend venues for experiencing live music, cultural shows, and local nightlife that celebrate Haitian music, like Kompa, and other traditional forms of entertainment.

Color Schema

The color scheme for this website draws directly from the Haitian flag, utilizing blue, red, and white to evoke a sense of national pride and cultural identity. Blue, symbolizing Haiti’s beauty and resilience, will be used as the primary background color for headers and footers, providing a cohesive and calming base that reflects the nation’s rich landscapes and serene coastal views. Red represents strength and vibrancy; it will be applied to buttons, accent elements, and call-to-action areas, drawing attention and inviting user engagement throughout the site. Finally, white will be used as a grounding element, ensuring readability and balance by being the main color for text on darker backgrounds and as a complementary accent around images and sections, creating a clean and inviting interface. This harmonious blend of colors not only pays homage to Haiti’s heritage but also offers a visually compelling and culturally resonant experience for site visitors.

Typography

In the typography section of the planning document, we have selected two fonts to create a balanced and visually appealing design: Roboto and Cinzel. Roboto will be used for body text, providing a modern and clean look that enhances readability across various devices. Its versatility makes it suitable for paragraphs, ensuring that users can easily engage with the content. For headings, Cinzel will be employed, lending a classic and elegant touch that draws attention and adds a sense of sophistication to the design. This combination allows for a clear hierarchy in the text, with Cinzel’s bold presence complementing the simplicity of Roboto. Additional fonts may be utilized for specific sections, such as quotes or highlights, to enhance the overall aesthetic and maintain visual interest throughout the website.
